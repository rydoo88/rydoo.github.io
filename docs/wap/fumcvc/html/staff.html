FUMCVC Staff with Biographies

Dr. Ji Seok Ju
Pastor
Ji@fumcvc.com

Ji Seok Ju has been the pastor of First United Methodist Church of Valley Center since July 1, 2015. This is his fourth pastoral appointment in the Great Plains Conference. He holds a Bachelor of Arts degree from Oklahoma Wesleyan University, a Master of Divinity from St. Paul School of Theology and a Doctor of Ministry from Wesley Theological Seminary. He and his wife Ara have two children Jeffrey and Jenna. “Knowing God,” “Growing in Grace,” and “Going in Love” is what God has called us to do. We invite you to come and experience genuine Christian fellowship and a foretaste the Kingdom of God..

Phoebe McCormick
Christian Education Director
Phoebe@fumcvc.com

Phoebe McCormick is the Christian Education Director at FUMCVC. She has an elementary education background and loves to teach children about the love of Jesus. Passing on the love of Christ to the youngest believers and supporting adults in their faith journey are Phoebe’s goals in life..

Mike McCormick
Director of Music Ministries
Mike@fumcvc.com

Mike is the Director of Music Ministries and feels that music is a wonderful way to help people praise, heal, fellowship, inspire and grow closer to God. Mike and his wife Phoebe have been blessed making wonderful memories with three wonderful daughters, three son-in-laws, and now four grandsons. His passion for the Dallas Cowboys is unmatched as proven by his saying: "God, Family and the Dallas Cowboys". Beginning in the fall of 2020, Mike became a Youth Co-Sponsor and works primarily with the high schoolers.
