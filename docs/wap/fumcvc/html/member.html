How Do I Join?

If you are interested in joining our church family, please let pastor Ji know. There will be a couple of membership classes to learn about the United Methodist Church.

Do I have to be baptized to join the church?

All members of the United Methodist Church are baptized Christians which means that when you come to join you may do so with a profession of faith which will include a time of baptism. If you have already been baptized before, at any age or in any Christian denomination, we recognize and receive your baptism and welcome you into our membership as a transfer from another Christian church. If you or a member of your family would like to schedule a baptism, you may do so by contacting pastor Ji by email or calling our church office. Pastor Ji will be happy to assist you in scheduling a baptism for infants, children, and adults.

Feel free to get in touch with us by using the form provided below for any additional information or inquiries. We appreciate your interest!
