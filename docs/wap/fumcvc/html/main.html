OIKOS

OIKOS “Oikos” is Greek word for “house” or “household” or “family,” but Oikos is more than a family and more than a household. It is the description for the church in the New Testament, the household of faith. For five weeks we are going to discover what God has in mind when He talks about family and when He talks about household as He looks at church community. Join us each weekend as we explore together on this term Oikos.
